The **Company Directory** provides a window into a massive global database of businesses, powered by TheCompaniesAPI (TCA). It serves as your primary tool for manual TAM building and targeted account research.

## Core Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Global Search" icon="globe">
    Search millions of companies by domain, name, industry, employee count, or revenue bracket.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Watchlists" icon="eye">
    Add interesting companies to a personal Watchlist without cluttering your main active Account pipeline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Instant Promotion" icon="arrow-up">
    Promote a directory listing directly to a first-class SignalArk Account with one click.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Industry Classification" icon="tags">
    Use AI to automatically classify uncategorized companies into your specific taxonomy.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Bulk Promotion & Preview

When you find a segment of companies that fit your ICP (e.g., "SaaS companies in London with 50-200 employees"), you can use the **Bulk Promote** feature.

Before any credits are consumed, the UI provides a detailed **Preview** showing exactly how many companies will be imported, how many already exist in your workspace, and the total credit cost for the required enrichment.

## YC Company Import

The directory includes special tags and filters for Y Combinator alumni, allowing you to easily build targeted lists of highly-funded, fast-growing startups based on their batch year and sector.
