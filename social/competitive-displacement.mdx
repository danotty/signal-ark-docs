When a buyer is frustrated with their current vendor, they often complain on platforms like X, Reddit, or HackerNews. SignalArk is designed to catch these frustration signals and turn them into pipeline.

## How it Works

1. **Targeting**: You set up Monitor Targets for your competitors' social handles, specific hashtags, or product names.
2. **Detection**: Flint monitors these targets for specific engagements (mentions, replies, or posts).
3. **Classification**: SignalArk analyzes the content of the engagement. If the post contains frustration keywords, negative sentiment, or phrases like "looking for alternatives," it is flagged as a high-intent signal.

## Integration with GTME Plays

Competitive displacement signals don't just sit in a feed; they actively feed into the GTME Plays engine. 

A strong negative post about a competitor will dramatically increase the confidence score of the following plays for that account:
- **Competitor Crisis Displacement**
- **Support Instability Displacement**

If the engager matches your ICP, this combination of social warmth and GTME confidence creates a prime opportunity to reach out with a highly contextual Why Now Brief.
