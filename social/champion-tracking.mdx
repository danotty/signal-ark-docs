One of the highest-converting motions in B2B sales is tracking your past champions—people who bought your software at a previous company—when they move to a new organization.

SignalArk's **Monitor Targets** allow you to do exactly this on social media.

## Setting Up Trackers

You can configure targets to monitor specific individuals or broader social categories:
- **X/Twitter Handles**: Track specific executives, past champions, or industry influencers.
- **LinkedIn Profiles**: Monitor when a champion posts about a new initiative.
- **Company Pages**: Track competitor announcements or target account updates.

### Adding a Target
1. Go to the **Social Signals** dashboard.
2. Click **Manage Trackers**.
3. Add a new target by providing the platform (e.g., X), the target type (e.g., x_handle), and the value (e.g., `@JohnDoe`).

Once added, SignalArk's ingestion partners will begin monitoring these entities. When an engagement occurs, it is processed, scored for warmth, and surfaced in your dashboard.
