SignalArk doesn't just look at firmographic data and job boards; it actively monitors the social web to catch buyer intent where conversations actually happen.

Our social intelligence engine tracks engagements across four major platforms:
- **X (Twitter)**
- **LinkedIn**
- **Reddit**
- **HackerNews**

## How Social Signals Differ

Traditional market signals (like a funding round or a job posting) are tied directly to an Account. 

Social Signals are primarily tied to a **Person** (the engager), and then resolved to an Account. Because of this, social signals flow into a dedicated `socialEngagementEvents` pipeline rather than the traditional market radar.

## External Ingestion (Flint)

Our social ingestion is powered by our external data partner, Flint. Flint continuously monitors your defined targets and submits batched social engagement events directly to our API (`POST /v1/social/ingest`).

When an event is ingested, SignalArk automatically:
1. Deduplicates the engagement.
2. Calculates a **Warmth Score** based on the type of interaction.
3. Resolves the engager to a company domain.
4. Checks the company against your ICP profiles.
5. Auto-promotes high-warmth, ICP-qualified leads directly into your sales pipeline.
