SignalArk’s dashboard is designed to eliminate "blank page syndrome" for sales reps. The centerpiece of this experience is the **Monday 8 Workflow**.

Instead of scrolling through hundreds of generic accounts, the Monday 8 curates the absolute top 8 most actionable accounts for your week.

## How Accounts are Selected

The Monday 8 isn't just a sorted list. It uses a composite calculation of:
- Recent high-confidence signals
- Strong ICP fit scores
- Active GTME Plays
- Recent social warmth

### Data Quality Requirements
To ensure the Monday 8 is actually useful, accounts must meet minimum data quality standards to be eligible for selection. An account **must** have both an **Industry** and an **Employee Count** populated.

## Interacting with the Monday 8

For each of the 8 accounts, reps are encouraged to make a quick decision:
- **Action**: Dive into the account, generate a Why Now brief, or push it to the CRM.
- **Snooze**: Hide the account from the Monday 8 for a set period (e.g., 7 days) if the timing isn't right.
- **Dismiss**: Remove it from consideration completely.

By working through the Monday 8 every week, reps ensure that no high-intent signal falls through the cracks.
