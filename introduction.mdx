SignalArk detects high-intent buyer signals across the internet and converts them into actionable sales pipeline. Instead of relying on static lists, SignalArk monitors your market in real-time, scores intent, and tells you exactly **why** you should reach out to an account **now**.

## The Detect → Stack → Route Framework

Our platform operates on a simple, powerful framework:

1. **Detect**: We monitor 15+ sources (job boards, news, social media, keyword tracking, and tech stacks) to catch active intent.
2. **Stack**: Signals aren't treated in isolation. We unify them into a single account timeline, calculating source diversity and compounding intent scores.
3. **Route**: Using Play Templates and GTME Plays, we route actionable intelligence directly to your CRM, Slack, or webhooks.
