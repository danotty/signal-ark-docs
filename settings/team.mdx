SignalArk is built for collaborative GTM teams. 

## Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Every invited team member must be assigned a role, which strictly governs their permissions across the platform.

| Role | Permissions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to Accounts, Signals, and Briefs. Cannot mutate data. |
| **Rep** | Can manage their assigned Accounts, enroll contacts in sequences, and action GTME plays. |
| **Manager** | Can view all team accounts, configure routing rules, and approve sequences. |
| **Auditor** | Read-only access to compliance, GDPR, and security logs. |
| **Admin** | Full access to settings, billing, integrations, and user management. |

## Team Workflows

- **Account Assignment**: Managers can assign accounts to specific Reps. Assignment locks ensure reps own their territory.
- **Approvals**: Certain actions (like sending automated outreach) can be configured to require Manager approval before execution.
- **Collaboration**: Team members can tag each other and leave comment threads on Accounts and Signals to coordinate account-based plays.
