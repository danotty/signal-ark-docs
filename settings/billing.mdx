SignalArk offers flexible plans designed to scale with your Go-To-Market team.

## Subscription Plans

- **Starter ($79/mo)**: Perfect for small teams getting started with intent-based selling. Includes 50 monthly AI credits.
- **Growth ($199/mo)**: Unlocks the Account Activation Briefs, Revenue Signal Proofs, and includes 500 AI credits.
- **Pro ($399/mo)**: Designed for scale, offering advanced integrations and 2000 AI credits.

*Annual pricing is available at a 20% discount.*

## The Credit System

Because SignalArk heavily utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) and premium external data APIs (like PDL and Exa) to generate high-quality intelligence, specific actions consume **Credits**.

### Action Credit Costs

| Action | Credits | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `waterfall_enrich` | 1 | Standard firmographic enrichment via TCA/PDL |
| `ai_message_gen` | 1 | Generating an AI outreach message |
| `why_now_brief` | 2 | Generating an AI "Why Now" account brief |
| `job_posting_scan` | 3 | Deep scanning job boards for tech stack signals |
| `brief_generation` | 3 | Generating a full Account Activation Brief (Gemini 2.5 Pro) |
| `lookalike_discovery` | 5 | Discovering TAM expansion targets via LLM + Exa |
| `bulk_scan_per_account` | 5 | Deep multi-source scan of a single account |

### Managing Credits
If you run low on credits, you can purchase one-time **Credit Packs** directly from the Billing page. All credit transactions are securely handled via Stripe.

When your balance is insufficient for a specific action, the UI will disable the action button and display a warning.
