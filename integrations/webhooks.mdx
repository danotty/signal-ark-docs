For custom routing, automation, or data warehousing, you can configure Webhook Delivery Destinations in SignalArk.

## Configuring a Webhook

1. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations > Webhooks**.
2. Add a new destination URL.
3. SignalArk will immediately send a `ping` event to verify connectivity.

## Payload Format

Webhook payloads contain a comprehensive snapshot of the signal, account context, and any matched plays.
