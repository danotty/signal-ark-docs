SignalArk includes a robust **Template Gallery** that allows sales leaders to standardize messaging across the team while still leaving room for personalization.

## Template Categories

Templates are organized by channel and use case:
- **Emails:** Cold outbound, signal-based follow-ups, and breakup emails.
- **LinkedIn:** Connection request notes and direct messages.
- **Call Scripts:** Discovery questions, objection handling, and voicemails.

## Play-Specific Templates

The true power of templates emerges when paired with Go-To-Market Execution (GTME) Plays. SignalArk provides predefined templates tailored to specific scenarios. For example:

- **Competitor Crisis Displacement:** A template addressing a competitor's recent outage or breach, positioned tactfully.
- **Executive Change Window:** A template welcoming a new CISO/CRO to their role and asking about their 90-day priorities.
- **Funding Expansion Blitz:** An email congratulating a company on their Series B and highlighting how your product scales with headcount.

## Deployment & Approval

Managers can draft templates and submit them for review. Once approved (`isApproved = true`), they become available in the Shared Templates library for all reps to use when executing sequence steps or manual outreach tasks.
