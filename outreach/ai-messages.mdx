Generic outreach performs poorly. SignalArk uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to automatically draft highly personalized outreach messages rooted in the specific signals that triggered your attention.

## Personalization Snippets

Instead of relying solely on basic merge tags like `{{first_name}}`, SignalArk generates dynamic **Personalization Snippets**. 

When an AI message is requested, the engine feeds the prospect's firmographic data, persona framing, and the specific **Why Now Context** from their Signal Timeline into the LLM. The output is an opening paragraph or complete message that explicitly states the reason for outreach (e.g., *"Noticed you just hired a new VP of Security and are expanding into the EU..."*).

## Usage in Workflows

AI message generation is available in two primary places:
1. **Manual Sequence Steps:** While reviewing a queued email task, a rep can click "Generate AI Draft" to populate the composer.
2. **Next Best Actions:** Automated recommendations often include pre-generated email drafts ready for rep review.

## Credit Costs

Because AI message generation utilizes advanced LLMs (like Gemini 2.5 Flash), it is a metered action. 
- **AI Message Generation:** 1 Credit per message.
- **Personalization Snippets:** 1 Credit per generation.

<Note>
If you run out of credits, the UI will disable the generation buttons and prompt you to purchase a top-up via Settings > Billing.
</Note>
