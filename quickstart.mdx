SignalArk is designed to deliver instant value. Follow these steps to seed your workspace and start detecting signals immediately.

### Step 1: Add Your Domains

The fastest way to get started is by telling SignalArk which accounts you care about.

1. Navigate to **Accounts > Add Accounts**.
2. Select **Paste Domains**.
3. Paste a list of up to 100 company domains (e.g., `acme.com`, `globex.io`).
4. Click **Import & Enrich**.

SignalArk will instantly run these domains through our multi-tier enrichment waterfall (TCA + PDL) to build complete firmographic profiles.

### Step 2: Review Your Monday 8

Once your accounts are enriched, SignalArk begins scanning for historical and real-time signals.

1. Head to your **Dashboard**.
2. Look at the **Monday 8 Workflow**.
3. SignalArk curates the top 8 most actionable accounts for your week based on recent signals, ICP fit, and intent scoring.

<Tip>
The Monday 8 requires basic data quality to function. Make sure your accounts have both an Industry and an Employee Count populated.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Connect Your CRM

Ensure signals turn into pipeline by connecting your CRM.

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**.
2. Click **Connect** next to HubSpot or Salesforce.
3. Complete the OAuth flow.
4. Once connected, you can push accounts, contacts, and AI-generated notes directly from SignalArk into your CRM with one click.
